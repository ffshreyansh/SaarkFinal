Dr. Anirban Bhowmick
Senior Assistant Professor

Review This Faculty

Dr. Anirban Bhowmick has completed his Ph.D from Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Mesra. He worked as a Postdoctoral fellow in Sound and Audio Group at IISc Bangalore where, he worked on the project of Audio Analytics funded by DRDO. He completed his M.E. with distinction from Birla Institute of Technology & Science. He has almost six years of teaching experience and has also served two years as an IEEE student branch counsellor at IMS Engineering College, Ghaziabad. He has published more than 25 papers in International Journals and Conferences. He is a reviewer of many reputed journals namely, Multidimensional System and Signal Processing, Applied Acoustic, IET Signal Processing, Computers and Electrical engineering, IEEE Access and many more. His research interest areas are Speech and Audio Processing, Speech Enhancement, Speech Recognition, Language identification and Audio Event Detection.
